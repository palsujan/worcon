Mr.Krishna's interiors

Living area

This classically modern living room is designed to be bright and airy with pure white walls and muted tones. Planter room divider and the landscape below the staircase enlivens the space with gorgeous greenery.

Kitchen

Every client has a different style, and wants to focus on adding a personal touch for organising things in their kitchen. For instance, in this kitchen the wooden tile cladding onto the wall blends in perfectly with the warm colours of the cabinets. With a personalised touch, you’re able to add unique elements to your kitchen. That’s the best part about having a modular kitchen, isn’t it?

Master bedroom

Enjoy the latest binge-worthy show from the comfort of your bedroom with a cool and cozy seating area. A plush, loveseat in a calming hue of blue makes for a sweet sitting area in the centre of this modern master bedroom.

Kids bedroom

A playful style In the boy’s bedroom enhances the youthful atmosphere with a soothing splash of teal which makes this bedroom pop against the backdrop of white walls. This design is inherently contemporary with neat lines and a compact structure.

Study Room

This compact study room layout is adapted to the users lifestyle and needs which allows to make the most use of the room .Aside from the desk and other work-related elements , this study room includes a comfortable nook close to the window for the user to relax, read and study.

Mr. Nagarjun’s Interiors

Proposed Interiors for a client who wished for Modern interiors.

Living

The decor scheme uses neutrals like beige, grey and white with wood (veneer) acting as the connector. “We believe using wood keeps one connected to nature; it gives a sense of comfort and cosines.” Double-height living space is skylit and connects to a mezzanine floor above. This vertical volume incorporates geometric panelling on the front wall that guides the eye all the way to the top. Wooden ascent and brass inlays further adds warmth and softness.

Kitchen

A well planned kitchen is that which ensures optimum usage of available space. This Parallel kitchen features two working areas opposite to each other and helps in easily segregating the tasks that you need to perform on each of the countertops, while moving in or out.

Master bedroom

A room that uses reflective surfaces and soothing colours to increase the sense of space and light. Lacquered glass, mirror, glossy deco finishes and chrome fixtures have been implemented to full space expanding effect in the entire room.
