WHO ARE WE?

G R Constructions is a construction firm that was established in the year 2010 by Mr. G Ramamurthy who is a builder & contractor. He has handled various constructions projects which where mostly residential and commercial and now in 2020 Mr. Ramamurthy plans on re-launching his firm as G R DESIGN-BUILD with two new members , his son Mr. Nithin G R who is civil engineer and daughter Ar. Lavanya G R, who is a practicing architect and interior designer.
