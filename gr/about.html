About Us

About

GR DESIGN

G R Design-Build was founded by G Ramamurthy, (Builder & Contractor) in 2010 . His son, Nithin G R ( Civil Engineer ) and daughter, Ar.Lavanya G R (Architect and Interior designer) have now joined their father with required professional degrees and experience to offer a one stop solution for all of your architectural and construction requirements.

With a core team consisting of a designer, civil engineer and builder, G R Design-Build is your perfect one stop solution for any construction and design services. They have a vast range of design ideas and construction solutions to offer, they hold expertise in residential and commercial projects.

From modern contemporary look to traditional and cultural oriented houses they have design solutions for all. Right from planning to execution and to handing over the project G R Design-Build has you covered.

G R Design-Build changes the traditional sequence of work which consists of the appointment of a designer on one side, and the appointment of a contractor on the other side. It answers the client's wishes for a single point of responsibility in an attempt to reduce risks and overall costs.
